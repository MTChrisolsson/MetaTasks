About MetaTask

A workflow platform built for teams that need clarity under pressure.

MetaTask was designed to unify process execution, scheduling, and organizational control into one operating surface, without forcing teams to stitch together disconnected tools.

Core stance

  • Process visibility should be native, not bolted on.
  • Security and privacy cannot be afterthoughts in operational software.
  • Growth should not force a platform rewrite.

Mission

Provide organizations with a platform that simplifies complex work while preserving privacy, accountability, and control.

Foundation

Built on Django, PostgreSQL, Redis, and modern web technologies for a robust, scalable service architecture.

Principles

GDPR-aware by design, role-based access control, modular services, and a system built to evolve with team complexity.
